Customizing

There are many ways to customize your mittens once you have the basic pattern down. Try some of the following ideas, or make your own!

  • using a different stitch,
  • adding multiple colors,
  • adding the cuff separately,
  • top stitching to add surface designs,
  • sewing in a fabric lining for extra warmth
Just remember, using different yarn weights, stitches, or linings will change the shape of the pattern.
